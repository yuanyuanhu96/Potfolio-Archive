Sherwin PRO+ APP

Introduction

We were challenged by our Client Sherwin Williams to redesign the PRO+ APP within 1 and a half months. It includes implementing the latest design system, reorganizes Information architecture, and adding new e-commerce and project management functionality. PRO+ is the name for our registered professional painter customer, they create 90% of the Sherwin Williams sales, and e-commerce will be an important growth point for the business.
