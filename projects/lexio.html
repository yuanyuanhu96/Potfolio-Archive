Sharing Experience of Lexio
- our business-facing data storytelling product

Introduction

During my five months in Narrative Science, I worked in the 5 people design and research team, focusing on the onboarding to the early use of Lexio, our new data storytelling product. This is a team project so I only included my contributions.

Team

Faye Sun - User Research
Yuanyuan Hu - Product Design

Image: my internship timeline


Background

Challenge of growth and adoption


With the mission of humanizing data analytics, we decided to use language as the media to communicate insights. That means we are very different from our competitors. How can we better communicate our product and bring unique value to the teams is a big opportunity and challenge for our retention and growth. The solution I designed directly contributes to the weekly retention rate and leads to long-term contracts.


Image: sythesized onboarding barriers

Insights

There is a strong need to share insights from Lexio.


  • Limited KPIs set in Lexio
  • Always need to double-check the story behind the data
  • Lexio’s strength in communicating, comparing to other tools








Design Challenge

How might we help teams seamlessly and accurately share Lexio insights and have successful conversations?






Let's be Human-centric

Will this idea solve user’s problems?


I sorted existing ideas and brainstormed new ideas according to the user's job to be done and pushed the team to prioritize features based more on user value.




Solution

A simple but complete experience both for "sender" and "receiver"
