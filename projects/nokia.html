Projects About Tools Resume



I interned in Nokia Software Venture Studio for six months, working on new product innovation. The projects I worked on help shifting our business to a high-return market. Due to the NDA, I cannot share the content of these ongoing projects.

How did my design capability help the team succeed?

First, I captured people's ideas quickly and visualized that by diagramming, storyboarding, and prototyping. For me, prototyping doesn't limit us to fewer choices. Instead, it helps us, our team, and stakeholders to see how ideas come to life. Then, we can have more meaningful and efficient discussions.

Second, I listened to and served our team members. My team members came from economics, engineering, and cognitive science backgrounds. Their refreshing thoughts and inspiring perspectives are the fortunes of our team. As the member with more design expertise, I also played the role of facilitator and knowledge broker to help people better landing their fascinating thoughts on the product.

Last and most important, I know how important human is and I advocated for that. Human factors are crucial for product success, but it is usually underestimated. As a designer, I always have users in my mind. I keep asking myself the following questions: how will our product meet the users? How will they interact? What value can we provide?



Prior to the experiences above, I also worked as a product manager for mobile APPs and worked on academic research projects about Human-Computer Interaction and Artificial Intelligence.

I really appreciate all these experiences. They enable me to think from many perspectives, to understand the whole journey of a product, to collaborate with different people, and, ultimately, to create desirable, feasible, and viable products and services for people.
