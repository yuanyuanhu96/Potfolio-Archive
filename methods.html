Welcome to Yuanyuan's design toolbox!

This place is to reflect on and share what I learned about design, it's just a begining and more will come :)




Double Diamond
Designers all know how to design by themselves, but there many times we should explain the process to others. This model may look dated but its widely accepeted and do its work.
Image source: https://www.testingtime.com/en/blog/double-diamond-process/
Round Robin Brainstorming
I really like this method for brainstorming because it force people to think through each others ideas and develop ideas together.
How can we let team member build on ideas rather than just criticizing is a huge topic. We also use the "Yes, and... No, but... " method.
Image source: https://www.mural.co/templates/round-robin
Heuristic Evaluation
Heuristic evaluation is very quick and cheap, but it can identify most usibility problems. The most common system we are using is from Nielsen Norman, I also like the "UI Tenets & Traps".
Image source: https://uxplanet.org/how-to-conduct-heuristic-evaluation-85548a355dca
Affinity Diagram
I was surprised that just putting similar things together can be a design and research method. After using it for many times, I found it is always a good start point to make sense of tones of qualitative data and finding patterns.
https://uxdict.io/design-thinking-methods-affinity-diagrams-357bd8671ad4
Paper Prototype
We have so many prototyping tool, from sketchy to Hi-Fi, do we still need paper and pen? Absolutely!
There is no copy and paste on the paper. Whenever I draw a similar interface it give me another chance to think about it again.
Back Casting
Limitation and current context are friends of designers, but its easy for teams to be lost in fixing endless bugs and finish the next and next release. Backcasting from future can help us clarify what is the most important things rather than most urgent -- they are not always the same.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UshLmJ5uW6U
Value Proposition Canvas
Once we have a good understanding of the user, this value proposition canvas can help us quickly come up with new ideas. While developing ideas, its still beneficial to keep it in minds and to keep our offerings align with user's needs.
Multi-Level Perspective
Multi-Level Perspective model help us undersatand an system from landscape, regime, and niche level. It also shows that we have different ways to interact with the system and drive change.
https://www.cairn-int.info/article-E_JIE_025_0211--the-inventor-s-perspective-on.htm
5 Whys
Many designer had the experience of given an wrong problem to solve. These Five Why help us undersatand what is the root cause of a problem also help our client understand what they are trying to solve.
https://online.visual-paradigm.com/knowledge/root-cause-analysis/root-cause-analysis-5-whys-technique/
